Everything you need to know about fast URL indexing.

Most URLs are picked up by crawlers within minutes and appear in search indexes within 24–48 hours. IndexNow-powered engines like Bing often reflect submissions in under an hour. Indexing speed also depends on your site's authority and content quality — we get your pages in front of crawlers fast, and quality content does the rest.

We submit through the Google Indexing API, Bing URL Submission API and the IndexNow protocol — which covers Bing, Yandex, Seznam.cz, Naver and other participating engines. One submission, every engine.
