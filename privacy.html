Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect your data.

Last updated: July 1, 2026

IndexJet ("we", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and the rights you have over it. It applies to indexjet.io, our dashboard and our API.

1. Data We Collect

Account data

Name, email address, hashed password (we never store plaintext passwords) and billing details processed by our PCI-DSS compliant payment provider. We do not store full card numbers.

Service data

The URLs you submit, submission timestamps, per-engine responses and project settings. This data is required to provide the service and produce your reports.

Usage data

API request logs (endpoint, status code, latency, truncated IP), dashboard interactions and diagnostic events. We use privacy-respecting, cookie-less analytics on our marketing site.

2. How We Use Data

  • To operate the service: validating, queueing and submitting your URLs to search engines
  • To bill you accurately and prevent fraud
  • To provide support and notify you about service events
  • To improve reliability and performance in aggregate

We never sell your data. We never use your submitted URLs for any purpose other than providing the service to you.

3. Legal Bases (GDPR)

We process data under contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b)), legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f)) for security and fraud prevention, and consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) for optional communications.

4. Data Sharing

We share data only with sub-processors necessary to run the service: cloud hosting, payment processing, transactional email and the search engine APIs you direct us to submit to. All sub-processors are bound by data processing agreements. A current list is available on request.

5. Data Retention

Account data is retained for the life of your account and deleted within 30 days of account closure. Submission history is retained for 24 months for reporting, then aggregated. API logs are retained for 90 days.

6. Your Rights

You may access, correct, export or delete your personal data at any time from the dashboard, or by emailing privacy@indexjet.io. EU/EEA and UK residents may also lodge a complaint with their supervisory authority. California residents have equivalent rights under the CCPA/CPRA.

7. Security

All traffic is encrypted with TLS 1.3. Data is encrypted at rest. Access is role-restricted, logged and reviewed. We run continuous vulnerability scanning and independent penetration testing at least annually.

8. International Transfers

Where data leaves the EU/EEA, transfers are protected by Standard Contractual Clauses. EU data residency is available on Enterprise plans.

9. Cookies

The marketing site uses no tracking cookies. The dashboard uses strictly necessary cookies for authentication and CSRF protection only.

10. Changes & Contact

We will notify account holders by email at least 14 days before material changes take effect. Questions? Contact our privacy team at privacy@indexjet.io.
